A Brief History

In 19-Oh-Four our glorious founder comrade Marshall Firskicsovich-Warren realized that his elephant had some soft peach fuzz all over its body. Firskicsovich realized that it was too hot for his elephant around the park near his home in Siberia.

So... he shaved it! And thus elephants became smooth.

With his Elephant weighing a whole 1kg less she was free to frolic and eat snow off of the ground in the park.
